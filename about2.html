The Compromise

After the end of the war the allies gathered together to decide the compromise to the end of the war. The president of the united states Woodrow Wilson presented his fourteen points. Suggesting that there was a good healthy way to address the post ww1 europe, however they were twisted by the British and French into the Versailles Treaty made to punish the Germans. The Germans were presented with the full war guilt. Forced to pay off the costs of the war. A payment that would not be completed until 2010 a little less than a century to repair for the effects of WW1.

The Treaty of Versailles not only forced Germany to give up territories to France, Belgium, Poland, the Czechs and the League of Nations, but it also included a War Guilt Clause forcing Germany to accept responsibility for the war, thereby making it liable for the damages.
